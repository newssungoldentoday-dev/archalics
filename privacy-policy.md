## 1. Introduction
Archa License ("we", "us", "our") is an open-source license protection tool developed in Baguio City, Philippines, in compliance with Republic Act No. 10173 - Data Privacy Act of 2012 of the Philippines, and respects GDPR for EU users.

This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Data We Collect
WE COLLECT NO PERSONAL DATA.

- No personal information (name, email, address)
- No IP addresses logged
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No cookies or tracking technologies
- No analytics tools
- No payment information (app is 100% free)

Our GitHub Action runs entirely within GitHub's infrastructure. We do not operate external servers.

## 3. How We Use Data
Since we collect no data, we use no data.

## 9. Your Rights Under Philippine Law (RA 10173)
As a data subject under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have:
- Right to be Informed
- Right to Access (not applicable, no data held)
- Right to Object
- Right to Erasure or Blocking
- Right to Rectification
- Right to Damages
- Right to Data Portability

Since we hold no personal data, these rights are automatically upheld by our zero-collection policy.

Contact the National Privacy Commission (NPC) Philippines at https://www.privacy.gov.ph if you have concerns.
